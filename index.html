What is consciousness?

Consciousness is the lived fact of awareness — the inward presence through which thought, sensation, memory, identity, imagination, perception, and reflection become knowable. It stands at the intersection of neuroscience, philosophy, contemplative inquiry, artificial intelligence, dream study, physics, and the mystery of subjective experience itself. This site serves as the central gateway to a seven-book series that examines consciousness from complementary angles, allowing readers to move from foundational questions to deeper and more specialized explorations.

Why this site exists

Consciousness Books is designed as a focused publishing branch within the wider CrownMind vision. It gives readers one dedicated place to explore the mystery of awareness through science, philosophy, comparative thought, dream inquiry, physics, animal studies, and altered states.
